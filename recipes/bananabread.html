Banana Bread

Description

There was a time that I had a bunch of bananas on the verge of going bad, and given that it was approaching the holidays, I thought I might try to make banana bread. Because, you know, I'm a fan of the stuff. Problem was, I started making it and soon realized that I was chronically short on ingredients. Not one to waste a bunch of ingredients I had already used, I did what came naturally: I panicked! But I also managed to improvise. What came out on the other side of the process was some of the richest banana bread I've ever made. Try it for yourselves and enjoy! Photo is courtesty of AllRecipes.

Ingredients

  • 7 large bananas, mashed
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup white sugar or Splenda
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 small box instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 1 tablespoon McCormick Breakfast Seasoning, or to taste
  • 1/2 cup melted butter

Steps

  1. Combine dry ingredients before adding butter. Stir until homogenous.
  2. Pour into greased 7x3 loaf pans until roughly half-full (fills about four).
  3. Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 1 hour or until loaves come out clean. Serve immediately for a toasty treat or chill in the refrigerator for a cool snack!
